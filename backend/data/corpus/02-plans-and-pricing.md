# Nimbus Plans and Pricing

Nimbus offers three plans: Free, Pro, and Team.

The **Free** plan includes 5 GB of storage, sync across up to 3 devices, and the
standard Markdown editor. Free accounts can create unlimited notes but can only
publish up to 3 public pages.

The **Pro** plan costs $8 per month, billed annually, or $10 per month billed
monthly. Pro raises storage to 200 GB, removes the device limit, and adds
version history, offline attachments, and priority sync. Pro is the plan most
individual power users choose.

The **Team** plan costs $14 per user per month and adds shared team notebooks,
centralized billing, an admin console, SAML single sign-on, and audit logs. Team
storage is pooled at 1 TB per organization plus 50 GB per additional seat.

All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial that does not require a credit card.
Annual billing saves roughly 20% compared to paying monthly. Nonprofits and
accredited students receive a 40% discount on the Pro and Team plans; discounts
are applied after verification and cannot be combined with other offers.
